// About

The person behind the edit.

I'm a Social Media Manager and Content Creator who helps creators and businesses produce engaging content across YouTube, Facebook, Instagram and TikTok — with a strong focus on YouTube Automation.

My work runs the full pipeline: researching viral topics, organizing scripts, editing video, designing thumbnails that earn the click, and preparing uploads that are clean and SEO-ready. I keep projects tidy in Google Drive and Sheets, and I lean on an AI-assisted workflow to move faster without cutting quality.

Whether it's faceless automation channels or personal brands, my job is simple: make content people actually stop to watch — and make the brand behind it look sharp doing it.
